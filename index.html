Frequently Asked Questions

How does QPin work?
QPin connects to your smartphone via Bluetooth. Use our intuitive app to select any image or GIF from your phone, and it appears on your QPin instantly. The process takes less than 3 seconds.
How long does the battery last?
QPin's battery provides 8-10 hours of continuous display time. It charges via USB-C in about 90 minutes for a full charge.
When will my order ship?
Pre-orders will begin shipping in August 2025. You'll receive tracking information as soon as your order is dispatched.
What image formats are supported?
QPin supports JPEG, PNG, and GIF formats. Our app automatically optimizes images for the best display quality on the LCD screen.
